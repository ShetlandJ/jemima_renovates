Southside

Location
Southside, Glasgow
Type
2 Bed Tenement Flat
Scope
Sourcing & executive furnishing
Yield
7.1%

The client was from Europe and looking to extend their current portfolio in Glasgow. A returning client after a successful previous relationship, they were keen to move quickly and didn't want any renovation or decoration done.

We found a two-bedroom tenement apartment that had already been fully renovated to an excellent standard in the south-east of the city. The client wanted a quick turnaround so we completed within 5 weeks. We furnished the property to an executive standard in express time, and it was reserved on the first viewing — the tenants willing to pay 6 months in advance to secure it.
