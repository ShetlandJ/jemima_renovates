Southside

Location
Southside, Glasgow
Type
1 Bed Flat
Scope
Full renovation & furnishing
Completed
January 2025

Every detail of this stunning one-bedroom flat in Glasgow's Southside was designed to make the most of a small space — creating a bright, inviting home, all on a tight budget.

A sage green colour scheme runs throughout, from the painted bay window frames and skirting boards to the kitchen cabinets and bathroom tiles. Brass fixtures in the bathroom, patterned floor tiles, and original stained glass fanlights give the flat real character. The result is a cohesive, considered renovation that punches well above its budget.
