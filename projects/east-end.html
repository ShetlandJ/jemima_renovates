East End

Location
East End, Glasgow
Type
1 Bed Tenement Flat
Scope
Full renovation
Yield
8%

The client was a couple from Europe whose son had studied in the city. They had several properties in another English city but were keen to buy in Glasgow. After several failed attempts to buy on their own — the distance making viewings impossible — they came to us with a set budget and realistic rental return in mind.

We secured a one-bedroom tenement flat that had seen better days with a very odd layout. After a full renovation to ensure the property flowed perfectly, the property was reserved on the first viewing by a lovely couple who were delighted with the modern decor.
