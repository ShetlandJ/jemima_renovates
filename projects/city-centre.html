City Centre

Location
City Centre, Glasgow
Type
1 Bed Tenement Flat
Scope
New kitchen, bathroom & open plan conversion
Yield
7.8%

The client was a young professional living in London who had been sitting on a small pot of money and knew investing in property was a good idea but didn't know where to start, let alone have the time to travel up.

We secured a one-bedroom tenement flat right in the heart of the city close to Central Station that had seen better days with a small kitchen but had potential to open it out. We embarked on a new kitchen and bathroom and created a very modern chic open-plan space. The property was reserved on the first viewing by a young professional delighted with what was on offer.
