Morocco Bathroom

Location
Glasgow
Type
Bathroom Renovation
Scope
Full design & renovation

Step inside the "Moroccan Mirage." A real vision that came alive with the right team — and a task and a half. Handmade zellige tiles in warm terracotta and cobalt blue are laid in herringbone and geometric patterns across the walls and floor, with intricate mosaic borders framing the space.

Brass rainfall and handheld shower fixtures complement the warm tones, while arched niches and a tadelakt-style plaster finish add authentic detail. A skylight floods the walk-in shower with natural light, making the whole room feel open and generous despite its footprint.
