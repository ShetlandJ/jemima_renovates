About

Jemima Spence

Having worked in the letting industry for almost a decade in Glasgow, I watched year on year the demand outstrip supply for quality affordable rental property in the city. A pattern emerged of professionals and students fighting over the same properties — stylish, good location, easy travel connections. These types of properties flew out the door every time.

I now have a client base of returning investors entrusting me with their hard-earned investment to complete the full package of sourcing, buying, renovating and furnishing. My long-standing team of tradesmen, estate agents and letting experts ensure a timely, value-added and efficient service.

I love what I do and relish in the tried and tested process investors and landlords have relied upon.

What I Do

From finding the right property to handing over the keys to a finished home.

Property Sourcing

Knowing the city street by street and experienced negotiating is a critical part of why our purchases are so successful. We run the figures with you before investing and break down why we're happy to propose a specific property.

Renovations

For any successful investment every aspect needs addressed. With the tenant's needs in mind, we advise on renovations, decoration and furnishings required — managed by an established team from strip-out to completion.

Lettings

We make the lettings process seamless, from ensuring the property is ready to let to moving tenants in. The whole system is tenant-driven so they're delighted with their new property and well looked after.
